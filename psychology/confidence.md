## Imposter syndrome

- high-achieving individuals
    - doubt their abilities
        - attribute success to luck rather than skill
    - fear being exposed as "frauds,"
        - despite evidence of competence.

## Dunning-Kruger effect

- a cognitive bias
    - experts underestimate their abilities
    - amateurs overestimate their abilities
- phase
    - overconfident start
    - reality check
    - informed confidence

## Layer of confidence

- rented confidence
    - posture
    - voice
    - presence
    - (strategy)
        - stand tall
        - take up space
        - breathe deeply
- earned confidence
    - self-efficacy
    - real but local
    - (strategy)
        - fill your gap and rounds you out
- owned confidence
    - self-esteem
    - seeing capacity while you are not able to do it
    - (strategy)
        - "Wait until you have some success or rejection"
        - "My action succeeded or failed. I didn't succeed or fail."

## DOs ✅

- take one small leap this weak
    - confidence is about actions
    - behavior shapes identity
- act till you become it
    - ask "When did I failed at?"
- confidence can be quiet
- self-compassion is the core power
    - be kind to myself

## DON'Ts❌

- fake it till you make it
    - confidence is not positive thinking
- fill confidence and act
    - confidence is not about readiness but about recovery

## Level-five leaders

- humility + will
    - modest but yet possesses an unwavering resolve
- ambition for the institution
    - not for personal fame
    - but for the company
- window and mirror
- right people first
- fanatical focus on results
    - high standards
